Lush greenery, perfect beaches and the calm tranquil of houseboats on serene backwaters – Kerala is nature's way of bestowing God's bounties on human beings. Allepy Packages

A backwater cruise on a houseboat allows you to see the scenic man made islands and beautiful sights of coconut fringed backwaters and paddy fields. You will want to book a houseboat in advance since they are always in high demand.
